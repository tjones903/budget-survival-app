💡 Real Money Tips

Cook at home: Eating out daily drains your money fast.
Track spending: If you don’t track it, you will lose it.
Cancel subscriptions: Small monthly charges add up quickly.
Use cash mindset: Spend like it’s your last dollars.
Flip items: Sell things you don’t need for quick cash.
Act early: Don’t wait until you’re broke to make money.
Follow the app: If it says you're at risk, take action immediately.
