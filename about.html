📘 About Budget Survival

Budget Survival is designed for people living paycheck to paycheck.

Instead of complicated budgeting tools, it focuses on one simple question:

“Will my money last until my next paycheck?”

The app calculates your daily spending limit and warns you if you're at risk of running out early.

Built to help you stay in control, avoid stress, and make smarter financial decisions every day.
