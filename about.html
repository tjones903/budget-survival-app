💰 Budget Survival Toolkit

This app is designed to help you stay in control of your money and avoid running out before your next paycheck. It combines budgeting, income tracking, and real-time decision tools to guide you every day.

📊 Smart Budgeting

Enter your monthly income, expenses, and days left to instantly see how much you can spend per day. The app will warn you if you are at risk and guide you on what to do next.

📷 Flip It (Make Money Fast)

Use the AI scanner to analyze items around you and get an estimate of their value, selling price, and profit. The smart scanner also helps you quickly decide what is worth flipping.

🧠 Life Mode

Choose your situation and get real guidance. Whether you are broke, hustling, stable, or in an emergency, the app gives you clear steps to move forward.

🎯 Track Progress

Track your wins, completed goals, and money made. Your progress is saved so you can see how far you’ve come.

⚡ Easy to Use

Enter your numbers and get instant results. The app is built to be fast, simple, and usable on any device including phones, tablets, and computers.
