📘 About Budget Survival Toolkit

Budget Survival Toolkit is designed to help people manage their money between paychecks and avoid running out before their next income.

The app calculates how much money you have left and how much you can safely spend each day, helping you stay in control of your finances.

💡 What To Do When Money Is Low

If your daily budget is low, the solution is not just to spend less — it's to increase your income.

Tap the "💸 Make Money Now" button on the home screen to open Flip It.

Flip It helps you:

  • Find items to flip for profit
  • Scan items and get smart advice
  • Estimate profit before buying
  • Sell items quickly using marketplace links
